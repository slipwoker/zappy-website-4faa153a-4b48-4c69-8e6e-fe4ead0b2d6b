Field Notes

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about contributing to, collaborating with, and advertising in KW Adventure Magazine.

01

How do I submit a story or photo essay for publication?

We welcome submissions from adventurers, photographers, and writers who live and breathe the outdoors. Send a brief pitch — no longer than 300 words — along with three to five sample images or writing clips to our editorial team. We review all pitches on a rolling basis and aim to respond within three weeks. Stories that combine personal narrative with a strong sense of place tend to resonate most with our readers. Please note that unsolicited full manuscripts are not accepted; a pitch must be approved before a full piece is submitted.

02

What partnership and advertising opportunities are available?

KW Adventure Magazine offers a range of brand partnership formats across both print and digital publishing platforms — from full-page editorial spreads and sponsored features to digital banner placements, newsletter integrations, and co-branded content series. Our audience skews toward active, values-driven readers with a genuine passion for outdoor exploration. We work closely with partners to ensure every collaboration feels authentic to our editorial voice. For a full media kit and rate card, please reach out through our contact form and a member of our partnerships team will follow up promptly.

03

How do I reach the editorial team for press or media inquiries?

For press accreditation, interview requests, editorial collaborations, or general media inquiries, please use our contact form and select "Editorial Inquiry" from the subject options. We try to respond to all media requests within five business days. If you are reaching out regarding an event, expedition, or launch you'd like us to cover, the more lead time you can give us the better — our editorial calendar is typically planned six to eight weeks in advance. We appreciate your patience and look forward to hearing about what you're working on.
